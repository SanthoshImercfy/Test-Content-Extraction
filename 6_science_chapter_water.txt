Answer Briefly
{ques}What is the water cycle?
{ans}The water cycle is the continuous movement of water on, above, and below the surface of the Earth. It includes processes like:
1. Evaporation
2. Condensation
3. Precipitation
4. Collection
This cycle maintains water balance in nature.
{next}

{ques}List different methods of water purification.
{ans}Common methods of water purification include:
1. Boiling
2. Filtration
3. Distillation
4. Chlorination
5. UV treatment
6. Reverse osmosis
{next}

{ques}What are the main causes of water pollution?
{ans}Main causes of water pollution include:
1. Industrial waste discharge
2. Sewage disposal
3. Agricultural runoff
4. Oil spills
5. Plastic waste
6. Chemical contamination
{next}

{ques}Why is water conservation important?
{ans}Water conservation is important because:
1. Only 3% of Earth's water is freshwater
2. Growing population increases water demand
3. Climate change affects water availability
4. Many regions face water scarcity
5. Clean water is essential for life
{next}

{ques}What are the different sources of water?
{ans}Sources of water include:
1. Surface water (rivers, lakes, ponds)
2. Groundwater (wells, springs)
3. Rainwater
4. Glaciers
5. Oceans (after desalination)
6. Atmospheric water (clouds, water vapor)
